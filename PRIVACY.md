# AI Widget Usage Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

AI Widget Usage is a local-first iPhone app and WidgetKit widget. The in-app name is AI Usage. It pairs with a Mac connector over local Wi-Fi so you can view sanitized Claude/Codex usage snapshots.

## English

We do not collect, sell, or track personal data in the v1 local Wi-Fi release.

The iPhone app may use:

- Camera: only to scan the pairing QR code shown by your Mac connector.
- Local Network: only to connect to the paired Mac connector on the same Wi-Fi network.
- App Group storage: only to share pairing state, widget settings, and cached usage snapshots between the app and widget on your device.

The Mac connector sends only sanitized snapshots to your paired iPhone: provider names, health state, usage percentages when available, reset labels, timestamps, and short status captions.

AI Widget Usage does not ask you to sign in to OpenAI, Anthropic, Claude, ChatGPT, or Codex inside the iPhone app. It does not send cookies, tokens, Keychain values, transcripts, raw logs, raw LAN hosts, raw provider output, or long command output as usage snapshots.

V1 does not use a cloud relay, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking SDK. If a cloud relay or analytics feature is added later, this policy and the App Store privacy answers must be updated before release.

Contact: superzhangkai@vip.qq.com
