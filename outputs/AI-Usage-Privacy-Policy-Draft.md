# AI Usage Privacy Policy Draft

Updated: 2026-06-14

This is a draft for the App Store privacy policy page. Publish it to a stable HTTPS URL before App Store submission.

## English

AI Usage helps you view local usage snapshots from a paired Mac connector in the iPhone app and WidgetKit widgets.

We do not collect, sell, or track personal data in the V1 local Wi-Fi release.

The iPhone app may use:

- Camera: only to scan the pairing QR code shown by your Mac connector.
- Local Network: only to connect to the paired Mac connector on the same Wi-Fi network.
- App Group storage: only to share pairing status, widget settings, and minimal sanitized usage snapshots between the app and widget.

The Mac connector sends only minimal sanitized status and usage fields to your paired iPhone. It must not send tokens, cookies, Keychain values, transcripts, raw logs, raw provider output, or long command output.

AI Usage does not ask you to sign in to OpenAI, Anthropic, Claude, or ChatGPT inside the app.

V1 does not use a cloud relay, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking SDK. If a cloud relay or analytics feature is added later, this policy and the App Store privacy answers must be updated before release.
