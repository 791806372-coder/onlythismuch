# AI Usage Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: `[TBD]`

AI Usage is a local-first iPhone app and WidgetKit widget that pairs with a Mac connector over local Wi-Fi.

## English

We do not collect, sell, or track personal data in the v1 local Wi-Fi release.

The iPhone app uses:

- Camera: to scan the pairing QR code shown by the Mac connector.
- Local Network: to connect to the paired Mac connector on the same Wi-Fi.
- App Group storage: to share pairing state, widget settings, and cached usage snapshots between the app and widget.

The Mac connector sends only sanitized snapshots: provider names, health state, usage percentages when available, reset labels, and timestamps. It does not send cookies, tokens, Keychain values, transcripts, raw logs, or long provider output.

## 简体中文

在 v1 本地 Wi-Fi 版本中，我们不收集、不出售、也不追踪个人数据。

## 日本語

v1のローカルWi-Fi版では、個人データを収集、販売、追跡しません。

iPhoneアプリが使用するもの：

- カメラ：Macコネクタに表示されたペアリングQRを読み取るため。
